Biography

Victoria Tran is currently a rising second year student at Tufts University studying computer science. She is from San Jose, California. Her hobbies include Asian American writing, water painting, traveling, and trying new foods. Victoria aspires to open up her own coffee shop one day. In the mean time she visits different coffee shops to gain inspiration and interior design ideas. Her activities on campus include being on a dance team called JumboRaas and being the publist for the Vietnamese Student Club.
